Our Story

Established in 1995, Renu's has been a cornerstone of authentic Bangladeshi and Desi cuisine in Cambridge for nearly 30 years. Our passion is to bring you the traditional flavours of our homeland, prepared with the freshest ingredients and a lot of love.

A Tradition of Taste

At Renu's, every dish tells a story – a story of family recipes passed down through generations, of aromatic spices carefully selected, and of a culinary heritage we are proud to share. We believe in a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

Join us for a dining experience that tantalizes your taste buds and warms your soul. From fiery curries to delicate biryanis, our menu is a celebration of Desi food at its finest.
